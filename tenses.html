Les temps

PRESENT SIMPLE

Le présent des habitudes, des actes répétés. Vérité générale.

Construction

Base verbale + (e) ou (es) avec she/he/it

Les adverbes utilisés avec le PS

each day, every day, sometimes, ever, never, always, often, usualy, rarely

Exemples de phrase positive:

I work everyday.

The sun rises in the east.

The train to London leaves at 6.pm

Exemples de phrase negative:

I don't work everyday.

The sun doesn't rise in the west.

The train to London doesn't leave at 6.pm

Exemples de phrase interrogative:

Do I work everyday ?

Does the sun rise in the east?

Does the train to London leave at 6.pm ?

PRESENT CONTINUOUS

Action en train de se dérouler, projet lié à un futur proche.

Construction

Auxiliaire be au présent + verb-ing

Exemples de phrase positive:

I'm working today.

The sun is shining

The train is leaving right now, hurry up !

Exemples de phrase negative:

I'm not working right now.

The sun is not shining yet.

The train is not leaving now./p>

Exemples de phrase interrogative:

Am i working now ?

Is the sun shining ?

Is the train leaving now ?
