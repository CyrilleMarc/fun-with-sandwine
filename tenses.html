Les temps

PRESENT SIMPLE

Le présent des habitudes, des actes répétés. Vérité générale.

Construction

Base verbale + (e) ou (es) avec she/he/it

Les adverbes utilisés avec le PS

each day, every day, sometimes, ever, never, always, often, usualy, rarely

Exemples de phrase positive:

I work everyday.

The sun rises in the east.

The train to London leaves at 6.pm

Exemples de phrase negative:

I don't work everyday.

The sun doesn't rise in the west.

The train to London doesn't leave at 6.pm

Exemples de phrase interrogative:

Do I work everyday ?

Does the sun rise in the east?

Does the train to London leave at 6.pm ?

PRESENT CONTINUOUS

Action en train de se dérouler, projet lié à un futur proche.

Construction

Auxiliaire be au présent + verb-ing

Exemples de phrase positive:

I'm working today.

The sun is shining

The train is leaving right now, hurry up !

Exemples de phrase negative:

I'm not working right now.

The sun is not shining yet.

The train is not leaving now./p>

Exemples de phrase interrogative:

Am i working now ?

Is the sun shining ?

Is the train leaving now ?

SIMPLE PAST

Action datée et achevée

Construction

base verbale + ed

Les adverbes utilisés avec le SP

On friday, last week, when

Exemples de phrase positive:

I worked yesterday

The sun shined last sunday

The train left at 6.pm

Exemples de phrase negative:

I didn't work yesterday

The sun didn't shine yesterday

The train didn't leave

Exemples de phrase interrogative:

Did you work last week ?

Did the sun shine last friday

did the train leave at 6pm?

PAST CONTINUOUS

Action qui était en train de se déroulé à moment précis du passé

Action en cours dans le passé interrompue par une autre action au prétérit

Construction

Auxiliaire be au prétérit (was/were) + verb-ing

Les adverbes utilisés avec le SP

yesterday, this morning, last Thusday

Exemples de phrase positive:

I was working

The sun was shining last sunday, when suddenly, it rained.

The train was waiting when in one second, it left.

Exemples de phrase negative:

I wasn't working yesterday when she came.

The sun wasn't shining last sunday during them marriage

The train wasn't leaving when they arrived in the station

Exemples de phrase interrogative:

Were you working this morning when the postman came ?

Was the sun shining last friday

Was the train leaving at 6pm?

PRESENT PERFECT SIMPLE

Action passé non datée ou au résultat visible

Action passé ayant valeur de bilan

Construction

Auxiliaire have au présent + verbe au participe passé (ed ou 3ème colonne du tableau)

Les adverbes utilisés avec le PPS

Ever, never, just, since, already, so far, for

Exemples de phrase positive:

I'm tired, I have worked a lot

I have already been to Marocco

Exemples de phrase negative:

I haven't gone to America

Exemples de phrase interrogative:

Were you working this morning when the postman came ?

Was the sun shining last friday

Was the train leaving at 6pm?
