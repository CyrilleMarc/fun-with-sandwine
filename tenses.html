PRESENT SIMPLE

Construction

Base verbale + (e) ou (es) avec she/he/it

Exemples de phrases positive:

I work everyday.

The sun rise in the east.

The train to London leaves at 6.pm

Exemples de phrases negative:

I don't work everyday.

The sun doesn't rise in the east.

The train to London doesn't leave at 6.pm

Exemples de phrases interrogative:

Do I work everyday ?

Does the sun rise in the east?

Does the train to London leave at 6.pm ?
